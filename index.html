What is Captive Drone?

We provide training, certification, equipment, software, data processing, and end to end support for in-house drone programs.

Years of FAA Experience

We have years of experience working with the FAA in both drone and commercial flight operations.

Industry Professionals

Our insider knowledge of utilities, construction, and aviation allow us to provide you with the best training and guidance in the industry.

Budget Conscious

As a small business, we understand the importance of saving money and being efficient. Our program is designed to give you the greatest ROI possible.

The Captive Drone Process

Step 1: Engagement

We work hand in hand with you to develop a package that meets your needs.

Step 2: In-Person Training

We provide hands on training for both the sUAS platform as well as the required software for your organization.

Step 3: Guidance for Part 107 Exam

We give you the instruction and training necessary to successfully pass the Part 107 Exam

Step 4: Develop FAA Complaint SOP

We work with you to develop a FAA Compliant Standard Operating Procedures

Step 5: Ongoing Maintenance of Licenses and Equipment

We continue to provide support for both new licensure as well as renewals. We also provide 24/7 support for your sUAS system and software.
