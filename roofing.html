Why This Program?

Our hands-on inspection experience in the Roofing Industry brings a unique perspective and insight into the challenge. This provides us with a keen awareness of the dangers and prohibitive costs associated with Inspecting Roofs.

Our Goal is to provide High Resolution, inspectable and measured roof data. We do this through our Software Platform that allows fully autonomous flight and data capture within minutes of any roof. This accurate data can then be passed on to insurance companies or clients.

Captive Drone provides cutting edge programs that implement the latest technology for safe and automated inspection and measurement.

The Program Includes:

  • Equipment (top-of-the-line drone)
  • Sensor (RGB)
  • Training (CFR14 Part 107 Course and Certication)
  • Software (learn more about our software partner EZ3D)
  • Customized S.O.P. and Safety Guidelines
  • On-going support and consulting

Trust our expert pilots and industry insiders to work with you to develop a highly customized drone program. With real-time support and annual refresher training, you'll always have the most up-to-date program so that you can be confident of FAA compliance.

The Software

EZ3D is a full software solution to quickly inspect, measure and document roofs with consumer drones. Automatically gather photos of the whole building exterior with a standard DJI drone and upload them to the cloud using our free EZFly mobile app.

Detailed photos are available for inspection and can be turned into measurements for repair estimation in a detailed, industry standard EZRoof PDF roof report or ESX claim file.

The Training

Training is conducted by drone pilots who are highly experienced in both commercial and military aviation. Our instructors bring first-hand experience to students to ensure you can begin utilizing drones to their full potential from day one.

Our training is structured to ensure success in both the FAA written exam as well as operations after you are licensed. We use both hands-on and lecture-based training to provide the best learning environment for new and experienced pilots alike.
