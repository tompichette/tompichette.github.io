Captive Drone Roofing Program

Applications for drones in all industries are growing at a rapid pace especially in the construction industry. However, few organizations possess the experience and resources to successfully navigate the FAA and source the right piece of technology to fit their business.

At Captive Drone you can count on our expertise to help guide you through the process of implementing a successful in-house drone program. From initial contact to sourcing the right equipment, through flight training and long-term monitoring of your licenses.

Why Captive Drone?

Many organizations in the construction industry are considering drone technology to help improve the efficiency and accuracy of their estimating departments but don't know how or where to start.

If your business is primarily focused on roof inspection and repair. Or if you perform claims settlement work for insurance companies and currently use programs like Eagleview or any type of 3-D modeling to complete estimates, then you should consider an in-house drone program.

Our top of the line equipment and software provides your business with a high ROI alternative to tasks that might currently be handled manually. Your drone can be programmed for an autonomous flight over almost any location while capturing hundreds of high-resolution images in minutes. Those images are then processed into a centimeter grade measurable and inspectable models, so you can confidently find damage and estimate costs, thus improving efficiency and decreasing waste while eliminating lost time performing manual inspections.

Our process ensures you will be fully licensed and flying in a matter of weeks by providing the following:

  • Training and licensing of pilots within the Federal Aviation Administrations (FAA) guidelines
  • Top of the line equipment & software
  • Customized Standard Operating Procedures (an FAA requirement)
  • Long-term maintenance of licenses, operating procedures, and safety protocols
