The Captive Drone Management Team

The team at Captive Drone brings a wide variety of experience to the table suited to meet the demands of several industries. The management team is comprised of individuals driven to bring value to our clients. At Captive Drone the focus is always around safety, understanding the ROI drones can provide and ensuring it is achieved in a safe and efficient manner. The bios below reflect our companies goals in achieving this standard.

Jesse Gibbon

Founder and COO

Jesse Gibbon spent several years serving as an aeromedical evacuation pilot in the military flying Uh-60 Blackhawks. His time in the military bolstered his love for aviation. Upon leaving the military he found himself diving into the drone industry using his experience as a helicopter pilot to navigate the FAA and utilize drones to their fullest potential.

Jesse spent 5 years in the drone industry providing quality drone services until he founded Captive Drone discovering that many companies can benefit from an in house program rather than paying for third party contractors. This realization has led him to build Captive Drone whose mission is to enhance safety, efficiency and reliability by integrating drones into any business.

When Jesse is not flying drones or helicopters he can be found in Acadia National Park enjoying the beauty of Maine.

Steve Whitney

President

With a decade of experience in business development and a burning desire to always create bigger and better things, Steve Whitney was a natural choice to lead the Captive Drone team.

Good business in today’s world begins with a good knowledge of people, a great attitude and a passion for finding the right answers. Steve shows the confidence and the charisma needed for the challenges of today’s corporate landscape. He brings his extensive experience and resources in business development and team leadership to Captive Drone. Along with partner, Jesse Gibbon, Steve is focused on building a team of professionals focusing on revolutionizing the drone industry. With innovative programs, unique training opportunities and the application of the latest technologies drone programs are made available to all business applications large or small.

Using the same focus of building strong relationships and forming partnerships with other leading technology companies Steve has helped mold the Captive Drone vision “to revolutionize drone assistance to all types of applications”. Captive Drone will change the way companies inspect everything from residential roofs to large-scale mining operations. Steve’s vision includes adding new industries that can benefit from the safety and efficiency and finding better ways to use resources currently available.

When Steve isn’t working you can find him on the golf course or spending time with his wife on their boat enjoying one of the many beautiful lakes in Maine.

Nate Kessman

VP of Sales

A decade of experience in the deregulated utility space and king of biz dev at Captive Drone.

Nate wishes nothing more than to live in a world where value wins over price, every fish is biting, and every putt is for birdie.

Nates career in retail energy began in 2008. In 2010, he took a position with Great Eastern Energy and immediately went to work building their channel sales division, overseeing double-digit growth which earned him a position as VP of Business Development. He then went on to work for Nordic Energy Services in 2015 helping to expand their service footprint in the northeast. In late 2016, he realized a growing need for specialized data management tools designed for retail energy and Integrated. Energy was born.

Through a partnership with UD Software Solutions Group Ltd., Nate and his partners now develop customizable software tools designed specifically for retail energy. Their first solution, Integrated Energy Console has just been released and is already making waves. In early 2018 Nate began consulting with Captive Drone to help spread the word of this Rockstar Startup leading efforts to improve business development and client experience.

Nate lives with his wife and two children in Hastings on Hudson, NY where you can find him working on the longest honey-do list in history, cooking, fishing or playing ice hockey.
